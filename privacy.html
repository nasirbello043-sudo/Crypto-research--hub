01 WHO WE ARE

Crypto Research Vault is an independent cryptocurrency research and analysis platform owned and operated by Nasir Bello, based in Kontagora, Niger State, Nigeria. The platform publishes fundamental analysis reports, research documents, white papers, and market intelligence content related to blockchain and cryptocurrency projects.

CRV is a personal project and is not affiliated with any financial institution, exchange, or investment firm. All content is for informational and educational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice.

When you create an account or use CRV, we may collect the following information:

▸

04 HOW WE STORE YOUR DATA

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase — a trusted, enterprise-grade database and authentication platform hosted on servers located in West EU (Ireland).

Your bookmarks and session tokens are stored in your browser's local storage on your own device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly interact with the platform.

Passwords are never stored in plain text. All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing before being stored. Even we cannot see your password.

05 THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

CRV uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

▸
Supabase — database and authentication. Stores your account information securely. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy
▸
GitHub Pages — website hosting. Serves the CRV platform files. Privacy policy: github.com/privacy
▸
GitHub API — used to fetch and display research reports automatically. No personal data is sent to GitHub API.
▸
Google Fonts — loads typography for the platform. May collect basic request data per Google's privacy policy.
▸
Google Docs Viewer — used to display PDF documents in-browser. PDFs are loaded from GitHub via Google's viewer service.
06 COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

CRV does not use tracking cookies. However, we use your browser's local storage to save the following data on your device:

▸
crv_session — your login session token, kept so you stay logged in between visits
▸
crv_bookmarks — your saved report bookmarks
▸
crv_files_cache — a temporary cache of report file names to speed up page loading (refreshes every 5 minutes)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or by signing out of your account. Signing out automatically removes your session token.

10 SECURITY

We take the security of your personal data seriously. The following measures are in place:

▸
Password Encryption — all passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
▸
HTTPS Encryption — all data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted via SSL/TLS
▸
JWT Tokens — authentication uses secure JSON Web Tokens with automatic expiry
▸
Rate Limiting — Supabase automatically blocks excessive login attempts to prevent brute force attacks
▸
Email Verification — all new accounts require email verification before access is granted

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential and for any activity that occurs under your account. Never share your password with anyone.
