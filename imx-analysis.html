01Project Overview
What is Immutable (IMX)? Immutable is the first Layer 2 scaling solution built exclusively for NFTs and blockchain gaming on Ethereum. It uses ZK-Rollup technology to deliver 9,000+ transactions per second with zero gas fees for NFT minting and trading, while fully inheriting Ethereum's security. The platform launched Immutable zkEVM — a fully EVM-compatible ZK-rollup chain purpose-built for gaming studios. Games like Gods Unchained, Guild of Guardians, and Illuvium are built on the platform. The IMX token is used for fees, staking, and governance — 20% of all protocol fees must be paid in IMX, creating consistent buy pressure as the ecosystem grows.
