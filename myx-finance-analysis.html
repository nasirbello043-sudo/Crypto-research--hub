01 / What is MYX Finance?

The Mission & Value Proposition

MYX Finance aims to democratize alpha — bringing institutional-grade perpetual trading to any trader, on any chain, with any token, without giving up custody.

Core Objective

  • Replace the traditional order book model with the Matching Pool Mechanism (MPM) — a pooled peer-to-pool-to-peer system that eliminates slippage by automatically netting long and short exposure at the protocol level.
  • Enable trading of perpetual contracts on any token with an AMM market — from BTC to the newest meme coin — permissionlessly and without manual bridging.
  • Achieve CEX-grade execution speed with a gasless, self-custodied architecture via Universal Account (UA) and chain abstraction, accepting collateral from 20+ chains including Solana.
  • Lower the capital cost of providing liquidity and drive down fees to ~1bp (0.01%), versus the 5–30bp industry norm, to attract volume from both retail and institutions.
  • Build a modular settlement layer for omnichain derivatives — effectively becoming infrastructure that other DEXs and aggregators route through.

🧠 Nasir's Analysis — Purpose

MYX's value prop is real and differentiated. The MPM solves a genuine problem: on-chain perpetuals have historically suffered from liquidity fragmentation, high slippage, and poor UX relative to CEXs. Chain abstraction is the right narrative for 2025–2026 as multi-chain fragmentation worsens. However, the "great protocol, struggling token" dynamic is already visible here too — protocol mechanics are sound, but fundamentals (revenue, fees, OI) have collapsed post-ATH. The mission is legitimate; execution and token design are where the real questions live.

Key Technologies

⚡ Matching Pool Mechanism (MPM)

A shared collateral pool nets long/short exposure internally. No order book, no AMM curve. Result: zero slippage, capital-efficient LP deployment, and funding rates that auto-balance market bias.

🌐 Universal Account (UA)

Two-layer wallet model — EOA/smart wallet retains custody, delegate-trader key handles execution. Gas abstracted via relayer network. Collateral recognized from 20+ chains without manual bridging.

🏭 Permissionless Market Factory

Anyone can list a perpetual market for any token that has an existing AMM pool. First perp DEX to offer truly permissionless listing — comparable to Uniswap's model but for derivatives.

🔮 Pyth + Chainlink Oracles

Real-time index pricing via Pyth Oracle ensures transparent, manipulation-resistant settlement. V2 adds Chainlink integration for enhanced reliability across more asset classes.

Reputation & Controversy
BMYX token controversy, KOL incident, airdrop insider scandal ($170M alleged), and wash trading allegations have all surfaced. Community trust is fragile. CEX-grade protocols need squeaky-clean reputations to attract institutional flow.
🟡 MEDIUM RISK
MPM Untested at Scale
The Matching Pool Mechanism has not yet been tested through a full bear market cycle or a true black swan event (flash crash, oracle failure). Long-term effectiveness of funding rate / rebate model to maintain balanced long/short positions is unvalidated.
🟡 MEDIUM RISK
Chain Abstraction Attack Surface
Advanced multi-chain architecture (20+ chain collateral, account abstraction) introduces new smart contract and bridge attack surfaces. V2 expansion to non-EVM chains like Solana adds further complexity. Multiple audit layers help but don't eliminate risk.
🟡 MEDIUM RISK
Competitive Intensity
Hyperliquid, dYdX V4, GMX V2, Drift, and LogX all compete in the perp DEX space with strong communities and liquidity. Hyperliquid in particular has demonstrated superior TVL retention and user stickiness. MYX needs V2 to be a genuine differentiator.
🟢 LOWER RISK
Smart Contract Security
Three rounds of audits (PeckShield, SlowMist x2) have been completed. Order-book-free architecture reduces certain manipulation vectors. Multi-sig controls in place. No major exploit to date — a positive track record for a young protocol.
🟢 LOWER RISK
Investor Quality
Tier-1 VCs (HongShan, ConsenSys, HashKey) provide operational credibility, introduce distribution channels, and have reputational skin in the game. ConsenSys's V2 partnership ensures at least one key stakeholder is deeply aligned with long-term execution.
