01Project Overview
What is Arbitrum? Arbitrum is Ethereum's leading Layer 2 scaling solution built by Offchain Labs — a startup founded by Princeton PhD cryptographers. It uses Optimistic Rollup technology to batch Ethereum transactions off-chain, cutting fees by 90%+ while inheriting Ethereum's security. The ARB token powers governance of the Arbitrum DAO which controls a 35.3% community treasury. Arbitrum hosts the deepest DeFi ecosystem of any L2, with $1.687B TVL and $524M in daily perpetuals volume.
